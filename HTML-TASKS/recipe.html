Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in water.
  2. Drain and keep aside.
  3. Cook garlic in olive oil.
  4. Add tomato sauce and spices.
  5. Mix spaghetti and serve.

Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • Salt and lemon juice
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables.
  2. Put them in a bowl.
  3. Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  4. Mix well and serve fresh.
